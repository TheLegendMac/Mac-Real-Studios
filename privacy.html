This Privacy Policy describes how Mac Real Studios and its applications — including Strobe Light For Watch — collect, use, and share information when you use our Wear OS and mobile applications. We are committed to transparency, minimal data collection, and protecting the people who use what we build.

01

Information We Collect Automatically

To provide a stable and safe experience for night runners, walkers, and active users, we use third-party services provided by Google (Firebase) that collect certain information automatically.

Crash Reporting — Firebase Crashlytics

If the app crashes during use, we collect anonymized diagnostic data about the crash — including device model, OS version, and the specific line of code that failed. This ensures the app stays reliable during safety-critical activities.

Analytics — Firebase Analytics

We collect non-identifiable usage data — such as which safety modes are most used and which colors are most frequently selected. This helps us prioritize features that improve visibility and safety. No personally identifying information is attached to this data.

Device Information

We may collect information about your Wear OS device type and screen resolution to ensure strobe patterns display correctly across different hardware configurations.

What we do not collect: We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any information that could personally identify you. All Firebase data is anonymized and aggregated.

02

Permissions & Sensor Data

The app requests specific device permissions in order to function. Here is exactly what each permission does.

Camera / Flashlight Access

Used solely to activate the physical LED flashlight on your device. We do not access the camera feed, capture photos, or record video.

Wake Lock

Keeps the strobe or flashlight active while you are moving, preventing the screen from turning off unexpectedly during safety-critical use.

03

In-App Purchases

All transactions are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. Mac Real Studios does not collect, handle, or store credit card numbers, bank details, or any financial information. We receive only a confirmation token from Google to unlock purchased features.

Note: For billing questions or refunds, please contact Google Play support directly.

04

Remote Configuration

We use Firebase Remote Config to adjust app settings — such as strobe frequencies — to improve battery performance and safety standards without requiring a full app update. This service does not collect personal identifier data.

05

Data Retention & Deletion

Firebase data is generally retained for 60 days to 14 months per standard Google Analytics retention policies. Because we have no account system — no login, no password, no user profile — we maintain no persistent user records. There is nothing to delete from Mac Real Studios’ systems. For Firebase or Google Play data, manage it through your Google Account settings.

06

Third-Party Services

Our applications integrate the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Google Play Services Firebase Crashlytics Firebase Analytics Firebase Remote Config Google Play Billing

Mac Real Studios does not integrate advertising SDKs, data brokers, or social media tracking into our current products.

07

Children’s Privacy

Strobe Light For Watch is a general-audience safety utility and does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us at macrealstudios@gmail.com and we will address it promptly.

08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights related to your personal information. Since we collect minimal data and maintain no user profiles, most of these rights are satisfied by design.

  • The right to know what personal information is held about you
  • The right to request deletion of any personal information we may have
  • The right to opt out of data sharing (we do not sell or share personal data)
  • The right to correct inaccurate information

Contact us at macrealstudios@gmail.com to exercise any of these rights.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our products evolve. When we do, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users through our website or within the app. Continued use after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
